Phoenix Hackathon?

Welcome to the third annual Phoenix Hackathon. Join us on December 9th to win prizes, attend workshops, and have fun

Hack-a-what?

A hackathon is a short 1-2 day event where students from middle and high school create and submit a project. Projects can be anything as long as it's related to STEM and follows the theme. During the event, students can choose to attend workshops to learn specific skills. The projects are judged, with overall winners and category winners being selected and awarded with prizes.
